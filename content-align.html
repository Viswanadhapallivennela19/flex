Property Applies To Axis Purpose Common Values
justify-content Container (flex/grid) Main axis Aligns flex/grid items along the main axis (horizontal in row, vertical in column) flex-start, flex-end, center, space-between, space-around, space-evenly
align-items Container (flex/grid) Cross axis Aligns flex/grid items along the cross axis (perpendicular to the main axis) stretch, flex-start, flex-end, center, baseline
align-self Individual flex/grid item Cross axis Aligns a single flex/grid item along the cross axis, overriding align-items auto, flex-start, flex-end, center, baseline, stretch
align-content Container (multi-line flex/grid) Cross axis (multi-line) Aligns flex/grid lines along the cross axis when items wrap onto multiple lines flex-start, flex-end, center, space-between, space-around, stretch
